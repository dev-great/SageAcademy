School of Product

The Faculty of Product provides diverse training programs that combine art, design, design thinking, and business in a distinctive way. The ideation, building, managing, marketing, and shipping phases of the full product lifecycle are all covered in the courses.

For those who want to begin and develop successful careers in technology without having to write code or go through fundamental technical training procedures, this school is ideal. The programs now offered to students are product design, product management, and product marketing.
